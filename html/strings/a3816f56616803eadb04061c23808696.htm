Original string:
Sometimes posts get migrated to another site incorrectly and need to be moved back. Clearing the migration history cleans up any old migration history from the revision history, unlocks and reopens the question, and undeletes any answers which got deleted due to the migration. **Warning!** This feature is destructive, and removes all traces of the migration from public revision histories. If you just want the "migration rejected" notice to go away, you can do so by simply unlocking the question.

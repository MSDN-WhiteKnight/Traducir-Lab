Teams on Stack Overflow are implemented on strictly segregated database schemas. Content on a Team can not be seen by anyone who is not signed up for your Team. That includes high-reputation users, moderators and even employees of Stack Overflow. Some employees have access to anonymous metadata about your Team, but that's limited under our terms of service and because we respect your privacy. It's our Prime Directive for Teams. Nobody outside your Team can see that you are a member. But there's no indication of where you earned it. We're always on guard against unauthorized access to private data. One step we take is to hire outside security experts who test our systems and look for vulnerabilities.
